ABOUT US

BUILT IN BERLIN

MONSOON FC was established in 2022 by a group of passionate footballers from Kerala, India who found their second home in Berlin, Germany.

What started as casual kickabouts in parks has grown into a competitive club with a strong community spirit.

AFFILIATION

UNDER
KEFF

MONSOON FC is proudly registered under the Kerala Football Federation (KEFF), one of the most respected football governing bodies for the Kerala diaspora community.

KEFF provides us with structured competition frameworks, access to tournaments, and a network of clubs sharing our passion for the game.

Through this affiliation, we maintain strong connections to our Kerala roots while competing in Berlin.
